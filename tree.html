Why TreePro tree surgery?

From single trees to complex multi-stem removals, our team uses industry-standard rigging techniques to ensure every felled tree comes down safely — protecting your property, your family, and our crew. We carry full public liability insurance up to £5 million.
Careful crown reduction maintains the natural shape and health of your tree while reducing its size, weight, and wind resistance. Our ISA-certified arborists understand tree biology — no topping, no butchering. Just clean, sympathetic pruning that keeps your trees thriving for years to come.
Storm damage doesn't wait for business hours. Our emergency callout team is available around the clock, 365 days a year, with a guaranteed 2-hour response time to your location. Whether it's a fallen limb on a roof or a blocked road, we'll be there fast.
